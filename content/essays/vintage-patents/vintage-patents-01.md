# US Patent 3,040,271: The original switchmode converter

* **Date:** June 27, 2026
* **Series:** Vintage Patents
* **Series Part:** 1
* **Category:** Concepts
* **Tags:** Power Electronics, Vintage, Patents
* **Excerpt:**  US Patent 3,040,271 is a foundational patent in the field of power electronics filed published on June 19, 1962, detailing an early implementation of a switching mode power supply.

---

 The patent describes a switching DC-DC converter that uses a saturable transformer to regulate voltage. This invention was a significant departure from the linear regulators that were common at the time, which were inefficient and generated a lot of heat. The switching regulator described in the patent was much more efficient and could handle higher power levels.
