# Power Electronics Engineer

* **Company:** dcbel inc. (Montreal, Quebec, Canada)
* **Title:** Power Electronics Engineer (Permanent Full-time)
* **Period:** November 2022 - Present

## Role Overview
Architect and develop power conversion stages, protection schemes, and safety compliance pipelines for high-power, safety-critical residential microgrid and Energy Storage System (ESS) platforms. Responsible for end-to-end hardware delivery—from simulation and schematic capture to compliance escrow, low-level firmware integration, and manufacturing scale.

## Core Accomplishments
* **Power Conversion Stage Design:** Engineered a high-voltage, high-density 14kW bidirectional ESS DC/DC converter using a half-bridge topology, active precharge networks, and economized relay controls. Sized and validated power components to support Locked Rotor Amperage (LRA) and a 10s power boost up to 20kW.
* **Solar DC Optimizer & Safety:** Designed a 400W-600W solar DC optimizer featuring active MPPT, rapid shutdown circuits, and Power Line Communication (PLC) telemetry links. Co-authored **US Patent 12,658,699** outlining the overvoltage protection control and rapid shutdown apparatus.
* **Forensics & Diagnostics:** Resolved false IGBT DESAT tripping during EV charger cable verification checks under light bleeder currents using first-principles diagnostics. Mitigated noise susceptibility at the gate driver sense node by synthesizing custom hardware gate filters.
* **Compliance & Validation:** Achieved safety and emissions certifications including UL 1741, UL 60730 Class B, and FCC Part 15.
