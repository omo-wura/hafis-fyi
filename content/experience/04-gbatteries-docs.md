# Hardware Documents Coordinator

* **Company:** GBatteries Energy inc. (Ottawa, Ontario, Canada)
* **Title:** Hardware Documents Coordinator (Documentation Role)
* **Period:** January 2022 - November 2022

## Role Overview
Represented the hardware engineering team on the company-wide documentation committee, coordinating standard operating procedures (SOPs) and technical knowledge transfer.

## Core Accomplishments
* **Knowledge Base Launch:** Launched a company-wide, markdown-based knowledge repository to preserve hardware design operating procedures and facilitate knowledge transfer.
* **Technical Authoring:** Published technical articles on the company's power electronics systems and organized the structure of the hardware team's design guidelines to streamline onboarding.
