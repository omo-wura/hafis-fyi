# PLM & Hardware Release Coordinator

* **Company:** dcbel inc. (Montreal, Quebec, Canada)
* **Title:** PLM & Hardware Release Coordinator (Governance Role)
* **Period:** April 2025 - Present

## Role Overview
Govern the hardware lifecycle release pipeline, manage the central CAD database, and enforce documentation compliance standards across the hardware engineering team. Act as the release gatekeeper between design engineering, quality assurance, and manufacturing partners.

## Core Accomplishments
* **PLM & Release Pipeline Governance:** Served as the designated Engineering Change Order (ECO) writer for the hardware engineering team, authoring and routing change packages in Arena PLM. Audited schematics, multi-layer board layouts, and bills of materials (BOMs) to verify completeness and adherence to internal standards before release.
* **Altium 365 CAD Database Management:** Managed the Altium 365 database library (DBLib), auditing footprints and schematic symbols to prevent layout errors and ensure component compatibility. Standardized component templates and parameters, enforcing classification standards (e.g., target voltages, tolerances, package sizes) to streamline component sourcing.
* **Manufacturing Handover & SOPs:** Coordinated the engineering handover to manufacturing operations, ensuring complete documentation packages (Gerbers, ODB++, assembly drawings, coordinate files) were delivered to contract manufacturers for NPI (New Product Introduction) builds. Formulated and documented extensive SOPs, layout guidelines, and design checklists to standardize CAD operations.
